Venturi Ground-Effect Floor Simulator

Station-based underfloor model with continuity, Bernoulli-style suction, leakage, boundary-layer blockage, diffuser recovery, and empirical separation penalties. The goal is believable aerodynamic trend analysis, not CFD.

The tunnel profile heights are treated as nominal geometry at the preset reference platform. Front/rear ride height, heave, yaw, and roll then perturb the effective tunnel gap in operation. Diffuser angle is used both as a display input and as an aggressiveness bias in the separation model.
